About

I am a theoretical and computational chemist interested in statistical mechanics, glass-forming fluids, jamming transitions, and complex systems. My work combines molecular dynamics simulations, Monte Carlo methods, mathematical modeling, and analytical approaches to understand the structure, dynamics and emergent behaviour of condensed matter systems.
